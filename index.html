About Us

As part of the 2020-21 UH Chemical Engineering Senior Design class, our group was tasked with designing a process for producing ethylene glycol. The work for this project was done over the course of a complete academic year. We leveraged concepts, techniques, and ideas from our chemical engineering curriculum

In the first semester of this class, we designed the process without the use of simulation programs. In the second, we optimized our initial design with the use of Aspen Plus.
